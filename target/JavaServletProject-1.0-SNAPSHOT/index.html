Post Map

What is Post Map?

The Post Map allows you to search by username or hashtag and displays a list of Tweets or Youtube videos as well as a map of where each post was made in the world. You can click on each pin on the map to see details on the post. You can also create a bounding box on the map and it will display a list of Youtube videos and Facebook posts in that area. It will also put pins on a map of where these posts were made.

Click to Show Guide

This is the general layout of the application

To search, type in the hashtag or username you'd like and click on the spyglass to the right, for example dogs

The next page will include a map of pins for posts in that area

Scrolling down shows a list of each of the posts

If you click a pin on the map, it will open a window showing information about the post

Some posts are in the same area, if you click on a pin it can spread to multiple pins which denote posts in the same location

You can also search by creating a boxed area where you want to see posts

Click the button "Create Box To Find Posts in Area" then click and drag your mouse over the area you would like to see posts from, then click on the spyglass to see results

The search will also produce pins on the map for posts in your selected area

You can scroll down to see the posts from that search as well
